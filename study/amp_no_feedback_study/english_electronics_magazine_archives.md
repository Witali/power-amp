# English-Language Electronics Magazine Archives

Last checked: 2026-05-16.

This note collects English-language electronics and circuit-design magazines worth reusing for future research. The main goal is to find practical analog circuits, transistor amplifiers, audio power amplifiers, radio/audio construction articles, and design notes.

## Best Starting Points for Amplifier Research

1. Wireless World / Electronics World
2. Elektor
3. Practical Electronics / Everyday Practical Electronics
4. audioXpress / Audio Amateur
5. Silicon Chip
6. Popular Electronics
7. Radio-Electronics / Electronics Now
8. Electronics Today International

## Archive List

| Magazine | Focus | Archive / back issues | Notes for this project |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wireless World / Electronics World | Serious analog, RF, audio, measurement, theory-heavy design articles | [World Radio History archive](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/Wireless_World_Magazine.htm), [current Electronics World site](https://www.electronicsworld.co.uk/) | Very high priority for classic audio amplifiers, feedback, distortion, filters, and professional analog design. |
| Elektor | Practical circuits, audio, test gear, power supplies, embedded, RF | [Elektor magazine collection](https://www.elektor.com/collections/magazines), [Elektor magazine archive](https://www.elektormagazine.com/magazine-archive), [Elektor Archive USB stick](https://www.elektor.com/products/elektor-archive-1974-2025-usb-stick-en) | High priority. Good practical projects with complete schematics. Official archive is membership/product based. |
| Practical Electronics / Everyday Practical Electronics / PE / EPE | Hobby construction, circuit design, audio, reader circuits, test equipment | [Official Practical Electronics site](https://www.electronpublishing.com/), [official legacy library](https://www.epemag.com/library.html), [World Radio History Practical Electronics archive](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/Practical_Electronics.htm) | High priority. Useful for buildable circuits and audio columns such as `Audio Out` and `Circuit Surgery`. |
| audioXpress / Audio Amateur | Audio electronics, amplifiers, loudspeakers, measurements, DIY audio | [audioXpress archive selector](https://audioxpress.com/select/audioxpress-archive), [audioXpress PDF back issues](https://cc-webshop.com/collections/archives-audioxpress-issues-pdfs) | Very high priority for amplifier-specific reading, including tube, solid-state, class-D, measurement, and distortion material. |
| Silicon Chip | Practical electronics, audio projects, service notes, test instruments | [Silicon Chip about/archive note](https://siliconchip.com.au/Help/About), [Silicon Chip back issues shop](https://www.siliconchip.com.au/Shop/Back%2BIssues%2B) | High priority for modern practical projects. The official site says online access can cover single issues, years, or the whole archive. |
| Popular Electronics | Hobby electronics, audio, radio, experimenter projects | [World Radio History archive](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/Popular-Electronics-Guide.htm) | Good historical source. Useful for simple transistor circuits and construction-style explanations. |
| Radio-Electronics / Electronics Now | Hobby and semi-professional electronics, test gear, audio, radio | [World Radio History archive](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/Radio_Electronics%20_Master_Page.htm) | Good historical source. Search both `Radio-Electronics` and later `Electronics Now`. |
| Electronics Today International (ETI) | Hobby construction, audio, instruments, RF, computers | [World Radio History UK edition](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/ETI_Magazine.htm), [World Radio History Canadian edition](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/ETI_Magazine_Canada.htm), [Electronics Digest archive](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/Electronics_Digest.htm) | Medium-high priority. ETI often published construction projects and audio circuits; Electronics Digest republishes selected ETI-style material. |
| Nuts & Volts | Hands-on hobby electronics, construction projects, education | [Nuts & Volts magazine page](https://www.nutsvolts.com/magazine), [Nuts & Volts back issues](https://www.nutsvolts.com/magazine/back-issues) | More modern hobby style. Useful for transistor basics, op-amps, and practical circuits, less focused on high-fidelity amplifier topology. |
| Circuit Cellar | Embedded systems, microcontrollers, hardware design | [Circuit Cellar magazine page](https://circuitcellar.com/magazine/), [Circuit Cellar PDF back issues](https://cc-webshop.com/collections/circuit-cellar-issues-pdfs) | Lower priority for pure analog amplifiers, but useful for measurement front ends, ADC/DAC hardware, and mixed-signal design. |
| EDN | Professional electronics design, design ideas, analog/power notes | [EDN Design Ideas](https://www.edn.com/design-ideas/) | Good source for small circuit blocks and engineering tricks rather than complete audio amplifier projects. |
| Electronic Design | Professional electronics engineering, analog, embedded, power | [Electronic Design issue archive](https://www.electronicdesign.com/magazine-digital-archive), [Electronic Design magazine page](https://www.electronicdesign.com/magazine) | Useful for professional background and component/application articles. Search the archive for `audio`, `amplifier`, `distortion`, `feedback`, `power amplifier`. |
| QST / QEX / ARRL publications | Amateur radio, RF, antennas, transmitters, measurement, experimenter circuits | [ARRL magazines](https://www.arrl.org/magazines), [ARRL periodicals archive/search](https://www.arrl.org/arrl-periodicals-archive-search), [World Radio History QST archive](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/QST.htm), [QEX page](https://www.arrl.org/qex/) | Useful mainly for RF amplifiers, measurement gear, transmitters, and experimenter-level circuit theory. ARRL official archive is member-only. |
| Practical Wireless | Radio and electronics construction | [World Radio History archive](https://www.worldradiohistory.com/Practical_Wireless_Magazine.htm) | Useful for RF, receivers, test gear, and older construction methods. Lower priority for audio power amplifiers but worth searching. |
| Make: | Maker projects, beginner-friendly electronics, microcontrollers | [Make: site](https://makezine.com/) | Low priority for deep analog work, but useful for accessible projects and construction hints. |

## Search Terms

For amplifier research in these archives, try:

- `transistor amplifier`
- `audio amplifier`
- `power amplifier`
- `class AB`
- `single supply amplifier`
- `bootstrap amplifier`
- `complementary emitter follower`
- `quasi complementary amplifier`
- `current feedback amplifier`
- `distortion`
- `negative feedback`
- `output stage`
- `loudspeaker amplifier`

For older British magazines, also search:

- `low frequency amplifier`
- `AF amplifier`
- `hi-fi amplifier`
- `transistorised amplifier`
- `output pair`
- `driver stage`

## Notes

- World Radio History is usually the fastest source for historical full-issue scans, but scan quality and OCR quality vary. Always verify important circuit details against the page image.
- Official publisher archives are preferred when they exist, especially for modern magazines and corrected downloadable PDFs.
- For transistor-only audio amplifiers, start with Wireless World, Elektor, Practical Electronics, audioXpress, Silicon Chip, Popular Electronics, Radio-Electronics, and ETI.
